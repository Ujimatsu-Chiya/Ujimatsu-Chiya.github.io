### Primitive Triangles

Consider the triangles with integer sides a, b and c with a \le b \le c.<br />
An integer sided triangle (a,b,c) is called primitive if <dfn title="gcd(a,b,c)=gcd(a,gcd(b,c))"> gcd(a,b,c)</dfn>=1. <br />
